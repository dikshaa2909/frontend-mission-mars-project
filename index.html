Mission Overview

ARES I: First Human Settlement on Mars

Stellar Voyages is proud to announce our groundbreaking mission to establish the first permanent human presence on the Red Planet. The ARES I mission will deploy a crew of six astronauts to Mars for a 26-month expedition.

Our team will conduct revolutionary scientific research, test new technologies for sustainable living, and lay the groundwork for future expansion of human civilization beyond Earth.

299 Million KM
26 Month Mission

Meet The Crew

Dr. Alex Chen

Mission Commander

Former NASA astronaut with 3 previous space missions. Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering and decorated Air Force pilot.

Dr. Sophia Rodriguez

Science Officer

Astrobiologist specializing in extreme environments. Led research teams in Antarctica and deep-sea expeditions.

Dr. James Wilson

Medical Officer

Surgeon and researcher in space medicine with expertise in long-duration mission health management.

Eng. Amara Okafor

Systems Engineer

Robotics specialist with background in AI and autonomous systems for extreme environments.

Dr. Hiroshi Tanaka

Geologist

Expert in planetary geology with extensive field experience in volcanic and desert environments on Earth.

Dr. Elena Petrov

Habitat Specialist

Architect and environmental systems engineer specializing in closed-loop life support systems.
